My projects

Mwananchi app

This is an app that gets views from the citizens. It is an alternative and modern way of public participation in governance.
The citizens can host discussions with lawyers who are willing to demistify the constitution of the land for the benefit of the citizens.

Katiba app

This is an app that helps citizens understand the laws of the particular country they are in. It helps in compliance and avoiding unnecessary law hurdles. You can navigate the website using keywords and the results will be up to date and elaborated by law practitioners.

Gikomba app

This is an online store that sells affordable clothes for all genders, body sizes and shapes. It is unique in that you can find a cloth that is as cheap as thirty shillings and it is durable. Knitters can also open shops in the app and sell their homemade pieces.

Safiri salama

This is a project that educates you on all the places you travel through. It can also play music based on the location. The app can also tell the history of the travel route using the music generated as the background music.

Ghetto uni

This is a platform that acts as a free online library for all resources published by kenyans. It aims to curate a one stop library for any topic. This is specifically aimed at decentralizing education and making it acccesible to all.
Especially Kenyan and African based topics.

Portfolio

This portfolio project is a home for all my projects. It shows the journey from a curious young guy in kenya trying to learn how to code to a semi novice developer who can develop and design websites. This is all thanks to The Odin Project.
